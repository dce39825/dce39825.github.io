A bit of my story.

My name is Chandler Eckler and I am from Fitzgerald, Georgia, which is a very small town in South Georgia with a population of around 10,000 people. It is one of those towns where everyone knows everyone and most people never leave. For me, I have always wanted to come to the University of Georgia and move away to live in Athens, and claim it as my second home. I was very community oriented growing up and was heavily involved in a lot of things throughout high school. I did a lot of things to try and figure out what I wanted to do when I got older. My first job was working in the community bank of Fitzgerald. Here I learned a lot about the banking world to see what it had to offer. After working here, I realized that I wanted to do something with business, and maybe one day have my own. My second job was working under my father who is a CPA and runs a partnership. This really opened my eyes and I realized that there were many options of jobs that I could choose to do when I got older.
